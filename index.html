No more filling out notebooks to keep organized.

Task Manager allows for you to create tasks and rewards. Both are assigned points values. As tasks are marked completed points are added to your account. Once enough points have been accumulated you can start taking rewards.

We believe that using incentives will make users more productive. Freakonomics offers various stories of using incentives to increase productivity. Using this concept as a backbone Task Manager was born.

By helping to fund Task Manager you will be helping to create a community of super productive people who know their hard work and will be worth it when they get to take their rewards.

Task Manager is different from most task management sites because it offers the user ways to incentivize themselves into completing their tasks. Users will have the ability view summaries of their activities on their personalized dashboard. The site will also offer insight into the communities total number of tasks completed and rewards taken.

Compete with friends. Choose common tasks and set the same point value for each task. Then see who is most productive within a given time frame.

Can you be more productive than the average user? Can you be the most productive amongst your friends? Task Manager will allow you to link with Facebook and Twitter to be able to brag and challenege your friends to be productive.

FAQ

Why Task Manager?
Task Manager's ability to use rewards to incentivize you will make you more productive than ever. Once you get started you will constantly be working to keep you available point total high enough to enjoy the best rewards you can think of for yourself.
Does Task Manager share my data with other users in order to create summaries.
Task Manager will require user authorization to share task and reward details. Without permission Task Manager will not share task details, we will only use statistics to show for totals within community numbers.
